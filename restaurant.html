J. Huston Tavern

"Serving meals to travelers along the Santa Fe Trail since 1834"
Been in Virginia since 1834, and still has the best pecan pie in Richmond. It’s so sweet, it will rot right through those wooden dentures!

Fried Chicken is the crunchiest you’ll ever eat, and very fresh too. Goes best with their famous mashed potatoes and gravy.

Woooweeee, I looooove J. Huston Tavern. The service here is amazing. My buddy John at J. Huston Tavern is the funniest and the best waiter I've ever met.

Two words: johnny cakes! The best there is. Besides that, the meal was delicious. It was served family style which worked well for our group, and the service was out of this world. Definitely will be coming back.
